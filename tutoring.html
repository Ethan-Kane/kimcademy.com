Tutoring

Weekly assignments for a broad range of subjects

· The key to writing proficiency is practice. I use your writing to help you improve·

How it works

1) I assign an essay writing assignment that you complete on your own time.

2) I mark the assignment and prepare a tailored lesson plan based on your writing abilities.

3) We meet and review your work, going over the areas in which you can improve.

4) Lessons are on a weekly basis, where I provide new and interesting assignments that allow you to practice your writing.

Lesson content

Core essay writing plan

Consists of 9 modules:
  1. Planning an essay
  2. Conceptualizing the thesis
  3. Writing the introduction
  4. Writing a body paragraph
  5. Writing the conclusion
  6. Role of language
  7. Gathering evidence
  8. Incorporating sources
  9. Citations and formatting

Common lesson topics

Covered during one-on-one lessons:

  • Vocabulary
  • Tenses
  • Conjunctions
  • Prepositions
  • Syntax
  • Sentence structure
  • Passive and active voice
  • Word types
