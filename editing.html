How it works

1) Send me the work-to-be-edited, along with any supplementary files such as a criteria, format instructions, etc.

2) I proofread and edit the work, annotating any changes I make along with an explanation.

3) I send back the edited and annotated version of the document, as well as a presentable version that is free from any markings.

4) (Optional) We meet and review your work, discussing any ambiguities or uncertainties I came across when editing.

What I check

Common editing areas

Some of the things I check for are:
  • Grammar
  • Syntax
  • Verb tense
  • Word choice
  • Word frequency
  • Sentence structure
  • Formatting
  • Citations and bibliography
  • Passive and active voice
